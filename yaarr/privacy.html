Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

YaArr is a native iOS client for managing *arr media services (Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr, and more). Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. All information stays on your device or is sent directly to your configured servers.

Server Connections

Server addresses and API keys are stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain and SwiftData. Your credentials are only used to communicate directly with your own servers and are never sent to any third party.

NZB Search

When you search NZB indexers or load saved search feeds, queries are sent directly from your device to the indexer you configured. No search data passes through our servers.

SSH Connections

SSH credentials and host keys are stored locally on your device. SSH connections are made directly from your device to your server.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at gregbuildsapps@gmail.com.
