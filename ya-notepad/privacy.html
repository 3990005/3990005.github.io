Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

Ya Notepad is a plain text editor for macOS. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app — it does nothing beyond editing text files on your Mac.

Data Collection

Ya Notepad does not collect, store, or transmit any data whatsoever. There are no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no network connections of any kind.

File Access

The app only reads and writes files you explicitly open or save through macOS file dialogs. It runs in the macOS App Sandbox and cannot access files without your direct action.

No Network Access

Ya Notepad makes zero network requests. It has no internet permission, no update checker, no crash reporter, and no usage tracking. Your text never leaves your machine.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at gregbuildsapps@gmail.com.
