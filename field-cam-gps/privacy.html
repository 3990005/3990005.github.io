Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Field Cam GPS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Data We Collect

None. Field Cam GPS does not collect any data from you or your device.

Device Permissions

Field Cam GPS requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely on your device:

  • Camera — Required to take photos.
  • Photo Library — Required to save photos and to select a custom logo watermark.
  • Location Services — Used to stamp GPS coordinates on photos. Location data is embedded in the photo overlay and is never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Field Cam GPS does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Your settings (such as job number, location, and overlay preferences) are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is never transmitted.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact gregbuildsapps@gmail.com.
