Field Cam GPS

Geotagged photo documentation for the field

Stamp every photo with GPS, compass heading, timestamps, and job info

Features

GPS Tagging

Every photo is automatically stamped with precise GPS coordinates. Know exactly where each shot was taken.

Compass Heading

Captures magnetic direction and degree bearing so you can document which way the camera was pointing.

Job Tracking

Assign a Job/PM number and location identifier to each session. All photos are stamped with your project info.

Custom Watermark

Add your company logo as a watermark with adjustable opacity and size. Brand every photo you take.

Multi-Lens Camera

Switch between ultra-wide (0.5x) and standard (1x) lenses. Front and back cameras with flash control.

Photo Descriptions

Add short descriptions to photos before capture. Text is burned directly into the image overlay.

Custom Labels

Rename overlay fields to match your workflow. Change "Job" and "Location" to whatever fits your industry.

Auto Album

Photos are automatically saved to a dedicated "Field Cam" album in your photo library for easy organization.

Privacy First

No data collection, no tracking, no network calls. Your photos and location data stay on your device.
