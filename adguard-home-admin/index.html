AdGuard Home Admin

Take control of your network's DNS

Features

Real-Time Query Log

Watch DNS queries stream live. Perfect for troubleshooting or spotting suspicious activity on your network.

Interactive Dashboard

View stats, 24-hour activity graphs, and top domains. Tap any item to dive deeper into your query log.

Client Management

Configure per-device filtering, blocked services, safe browsing, and parental controls for each client.

Filter Control

Manage blocklists, add custom rules, configure DNS rewrites, and unblock domains with a tap.

Multi-Server Support

Connect to multiple AdGuard Home servers and switch between them seamlessly.

Secure

Credentials stored securely in iOS Keychain. Supports HTTPS connections.
