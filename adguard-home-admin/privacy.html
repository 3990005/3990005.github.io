Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Overview

AdGuard Home Admin is a remote control application for AdGuard Home DNS servers. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. All information stays on your device or is sent directly to your configured AdGuard Home server.

Server Connection

Server addresses and credentials are stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain and are never sent to any third party. Your credentials are only used to communicate directly with your AdGuard Home server.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information as described in their privacy policy. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at gregbuildsapps@gmail.com.
