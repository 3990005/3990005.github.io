Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

NZBgk is a native iOS client for browsing and searching the NZBGeek Usenet indexer. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. All information stays on your device or is sent directly to NZBGeek and TMDB as described below.

Server Connection

Your NZBGeek API key is stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain and is never sent to any third party. Your API key is only used to communicate directly with the NZBGeek API. Search queries, saved seek feeds, and cart actions are sent directly to NZBGeek's servers.

TMDB Integration

If you provide a TMDB API key, the app will fetch movie and TV metadata (posters, ratings, synopses) directly from The Movie Database (TMDB). Your TMDB API key is stored in iOS Keychain and is only used to communicate with TMDB's API.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at gregbuildsapps@gmail.com.
