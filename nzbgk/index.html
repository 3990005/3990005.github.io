Browse the full NZBGeek index. Filter by category, sort by date, size, name, or downloads, and limit results by age.

Add items to your NZBGeek cart with a swipe. View, remove, and share items without opening a browser.

Secure

API keys stored securely in iOS Keychain. Your credentials never leave your device.
